Overview

Our sentiment analysis web application allows you to explore the public mood on social media posts from YouTube, Instagram, and Reddit. Simply select a platform, enter the post URL, and our system will analyze the comments to classify them as Positive, Neutral, or Negative. Results are displayed in an easy to read bar chart, showing the sentiment breakdown, and a word cloud highlights the most frequently mentioned topics. This tool provides a quick, visual way to understand audience reactions and key discussion points on popular content.
